Winter Europe trip plan

Paris

We'll be arriving at Paris first, we will probably stay here from between the 24th of December to the 30th of December.

These are some places that we could visit in Paris (Northern France):

  • Louvre
  • The Eiffel Tower
  • Monmart
  • Rouen
  • Versailles

München

After Paris, we'll take the eurorail to visit München and stay over there till the 6th of January.

These are some places that we could visit in München as well as Bayern:

  • Allianz Arena
  • Neuschwanstein Castle
  • StadtZentrum
  • Marketplatz
  • Augsburg
  • Stuttgart

Warsaw

We'll be ending the trip in Poland, we'll stay there till the 13th of January.

These are some places that we could visit in Warszawa and Gdańsk:

  • Warszawa Uprising museum
  • Centrum Nauki Kopernik
  • Wilanów Palace
  • Gdańsk
  • Sopot
  • Monte Cassino
  • Malbork Castle
